About Us


Our Mission

Send a rocket to mars. Naa just kidding :D ArtShop.com is a platform for artists to showcase their talent to the world and earn from it.
If you are an post, Artshop lets you find buyers for your art and if you are an art lover, you get to buy from a wide range of art pieces and a wide range of artists.

We believe in a beautiful world where the post and the buyers talk to each other. And the buyer buys directly from the artists. Some may say that we do not like to be dragged into all the hustle of payment, storing and delivery of art. Well they are right :)


What we do

Our platform is for two kinds of people. One that understands art and the other, who create them.

We let the artists showcase their art by putting a price tag on it. The post decides the rate on the tag.

If you like the work, you can buy it from the post. We let the post know that you are interested in buying his work. The post is informed and arranges for the payment and transportation of the art

Artshop.com does not handle the payment, storage and delivery of the art. These are done directly between the post and the buyer


How we earn

We charge the post a nominal charge for showcasing their art on our platform(Artshop.com). Also, we sell ads. To advertise please contact our developer
