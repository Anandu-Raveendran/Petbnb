What you should do

These are a few things to keep in mind

Upload pictures

Upload good quality images on to the gallery.

Add the details of the art

Be specific. Take the time to fill the accurate details for the art.

Set the price right

Set the price of the images reasonably. Buyers might back off if its too costly.

Buyer calls

Talk to the buyer interested to buy the art about the date, delivery and payment. Artshop is not liable

Packaging

Package the Art appropriately. Any damage and the buyer can reject your Art. Artshop is not liable.

Communication is key

Artshop is not liable for packaging and delivery hence we insist you to communicate well with buyers.

Deliver the Art

We do not take care of the delivery of the Art. It would be on your end. Talk to the buyer for delivery location, etc.

Make sure the Buyer is happy

Make sure the Art delivered has no defects and is as expected. The buyer has the right to reject if so.

Payment

The payment shall be done by the buyer directly to the post. Artshop is not involved.
